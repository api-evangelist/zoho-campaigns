At some point, most of us have looked at our screens and felt genuinely tired. Not from the work itself, but from everything around it; the endless tabs, the notifications that never stop, and a bunch of apps claiming to make life easier. You download one to stay organized, another to stay focused, and yet another to manage the first two.
